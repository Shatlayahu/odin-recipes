Sour Cream Cake


    This delightful cake is made with sour cream, offering a rich and creamy texture reminiscent 
    of cheesecake. Despite its indulgent flavor and velvety consistency, it remains low in calories, 
    making it a perfect guilt-free treat for any occasion. 
    
    Enjoy the best of both worlds with this luscious yet light dessert!

Ingredients

Cake base

  • 300 grams all-purpose of flour
  • 200 grams butter
  • 60 grams granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs

Topping

  • 500 grams sour-cream
  • 70 grams granulated sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla sugar
  • 2 tbsp corn starch (if your sour-cream is too liquid)

Steps

Cake base

  1. Mix all the ingredients except for 2 eggs.
  2. Put eggs and gently mix it.
  3. Cool the mixture in fridge for 15 minutes.

Topping

  1. Mix all the ingredients.
        After your dough has chilled sufficiently, press it into the cake pan.
	Use a fork to poke holes all over the base. Pour the liquid mixture over the prepared base.
	Tap the pan 2-3 times to release any air bubbles.
	Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for 35 minutes.
	
        Cool the cake in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours before serving.
